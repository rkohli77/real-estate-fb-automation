Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Quick Summary: We only use your Facebook data to post content when you authorize it. We don't sell your information or store it longer than necessary.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Real Estate Sage application and connect it with Facebook, we may collect:

  • Basic profile information (name, email address)
  • Facebook page information (if you manage pages)
  • Content you choose to post through our application
  • Usage data to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide our real estate social media posting service
  • Post content to your Facebook account when you authorize it
  • Improve our application and user experience
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy. We may share information:

  • With Facebook when you authorize posts
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With service providers who help us operate our application (under strict confidentiality)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

5. Facebook Integration

When you connect your Facebook account, you grant us permission to post on your behalf. Important details:

  • You can revoke this permission at any time through your Facebook settings
  • We only post content that you explicitly approve
  • We comply with Facebook's Platform Policy and Data Policy
  • We do not access private messages or personal posts

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Account information: Until you delete your account
  • Posted content logs: 90 days for troubleshooting
  • Facebook tokens: Refreshed automatically, expired tokens deleted immediately

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze how our service is used
  • Improve user experience

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification for significant changes
  • Displaying a notice in our application
